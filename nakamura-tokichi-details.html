Nakamura Tokichi

4.9 (500)

​Nakamura Tokichi is a renowned tea shop and cultural landmark located in Uji, Kyoto, celebrated for its exquisite selection of high-quality Japanese teas, particularly Uji matcha.​ Established in 1819, this historic establishment has built a legacy of craftsmanship and dedication to the art of tea. Visitors can enjoy a serene atmosphere while sampling a variety of traditional tea offerings, including the signature Nakamura-cha blend, which embodies the rich flavors of Uji tea. The shop also features a café where guests can savor matcha-infused sweets and light meals, highlighting the versatility of this beloved ingredient. With its commitment to preserving tea culture and providing an immersive experience, Nakamura Tokichi serves as a perfect destination for tea enthusiasts and those looking to explore the rich heritage of Japanese tea in a beautiful setting.

Details

Interest/Topic: Food & Drink

Popularity: High

Reservation: Not Required

Estimated Price Range: Matcha desserts range from ¥500–¥1,500 ($3–$10), while traditional tea sets can be around ¥1,000 ($7).

Things to Do

Tips

  1. Highlights: Traditional tea experience, signature matcha, cultural heritage.
  2. Duration: Visits typically last between one to two hours, depending on the chosen tea experience and any additional dining options.
  • Traditional Tea Experience: Enjoy authentic Japanese tea ceremonies that highlight the art of matcha preparation in a serene atmosphere.
  • Signature Matcha: Savor high-quality matcha sourced from Uji, along with traditional wagashi (Japanese sweets) that enhance the tasting experience.
  • Cultural Heritage: With over 160 years of history, Nakamura Tokichi reflects Kyoto’s rich tea culture, blending time-honored traditions with modern aesthetics.
  • Make Reservations: Book in advance, especially during busy seasons, to secure your spot.
  • Arrive Early: Get to the venue a bit early to appreciate the ambiance.
  • Explore Nearby: After your visit, check out nearby attractions like Gion or the philosophy path for a full cultural experience.
