Nestled in the heart of Japan, Kyoto is a harmonious blend of ancient tradition and modern charm. Once the imperial capital, the city is a treasure trove of historical sites, serene gardens, and vibrant culture. Whether you're a history buff, a foodie, or a nature lover, Kyoto offers something for everyone.

There are so many things to do in Kyoto such as exploring, eating local cuisine, relaxing, and more! Here is a list of some options you can do on a trip:

  1. Explore the Gion District: Wander through Kyoto‚Äôs historic geisha district, filled with traditional tea houses and wooden machiya homes.
  2. Stroll Through Pontocho: Discover one of Kyoto's most picturesque and atmospheric streets. This narrow alley is lined with traditional wooden buildings and offers plenty of dining options, from upscale kaiseki to cozy izakayas.
  3. Relax at Gion Starbucks: Enjoy a cup of coffee at a special Starbucks location nestled in a traditional Kyoto building. This store blends modern coffee culture with the historic aesthetics of Gion.
  4. Learn at Chifumi Calligraphy: Try your hand at Japanese calligraphy in a traditional setting, guided by a master.
  5. Experience a Camellia Tea Ceremony: Deepen your understanding of Japanese culture by participating in a traditional tea ceremony with a focus on camellia flowers.
  6. Walk the Philosopher's Path: Take a serene stroll along this scenic canal path, famous for its cherry blossoms and peaceful ambiance.
