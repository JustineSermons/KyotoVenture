Things to Do

Tips

  1. Highlights: Cherry blossoms, historic temples, and traditional shops.
  2. Duration: Year-round, with peak visits during cherry blossom season in early April. Hanami (flower viewing) celebrations in spring.
  • Stroll the Canal: Walk the scenic stone path lined with cherry trees.
  • Visit Temples: Explore Ginkaku-ji and Honen-in.
  • Join Hanami: Enjoy cherry blossom festivals.
  • Shop & Dine: Find unique shops and cafes.
  • Take Photos: Snap seasonal views and historic sites.
  • Arrive Early: Enjoy a quieter path in the morning.
  • Time Your Visit: Check spring bloom forecasts.
  • Wear Comfy Shoes: Prepare for uneven paths.
  • Carry Cash: Smaller shops may not take cards.
  • Follow Etiquette: Be mindful during busy times.
  • Check Weather: Dress for the season and bring an umbrella.
