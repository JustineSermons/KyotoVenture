Kyoto Questions

What are the most common transportation options? ▼

Answer: The most common transportation options are by train, bus or taxi. The most common way of transportation is by train

When is the best time to visit Kyoto? ▼

Answer: The best time to visit Kyoto is typically around mid-March to May during Cherry blossom Season and October to November for the Autumn Foliage.

What are the most popular attractions? ▼

Answer: The most popular and well-known attractions are the Fushimi Inari Shrine, Kinkakuji Temple, and Kiyomizu-dera Temple.

Do I need to know Japanese when going to Kyoto? ▼

Answer: While knowing some Japanese can enhance your experience, many popular tourist areas, restaurants, and hotels have English-Speaking Staff, making it easy to Navigate without fluent Japanese.

Is Kyoto safe for solo travelers? ▼

Answer: Yes! Kyoto is very safe for solo travelers, including women. The city has very low crime rates and a welcoming atmosphere. However, going to any destination is always best to practice common safety precautions.

Are there any special events or festivals in Kyoto? ▼

Answer: There are many festivals and special events in Kyoto. Many of these festivals and events showcase Kyoto’s rich cultural heritage.

Are there any cultural etiquette tips I should be aware of? ▼

Answer: Yes there are a couple practices to keep in mind that i nclude respecting temples and shrines and taking shoes off in traditional ryokans, homes, or indoors in certain places.

Do I need to book attractions in advance? ▼

Answer: It is advisable to book popular attractions in advance, especially during certain seasons where there is a peak of tourist population. Reservations are often required for special dining experiences, tours, and tea ceramonies.

KyotoVenture Questions

How can I create my own Kyoto itinerary? ▼

Answer: You can go to your profile and select the "Create an Itinerary" option to create your own Kyoto itinerary. From there you can select how many days you want in your collection (Up to a maximum of 14 per itinerary collection) and start adding activities to different days by browsing our Activities page. Remember to set a default itinerary for a collection you make so that activities you add can automatically go into that collection when clicking "Add to Itinerary" for any activity. Otherwise you will be prompted to choose a collection every time you click "Add to Itinerary". You can always change the dates and times of an activity later on when selecting the "Edit Itinerary" option.

How do I book accommodations? ▼

Answer: You can book accommodations by going to Itineraries on the navigation menu and then selecting Accommodations as one of the drop downs. From there you can browse different accommodations and sort them by area, price, and accommodation type to find one that works best for you!

How does payments work when booking?▼

Answer: When booking anything for accommodation or transportation you have to select the required button option such as "Book Accommodation" or "Book Transportation" which will direct you to a third party site where you can then continue with the booking and payment.

Can I cancel or modify my booking? ▼

Answer: Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking by going to your profile and going to the "My Accommodations" section. Cancellation policies vary depending on the accommodation so be sure to check the specific terms of your booking.

How can I contact customer support? ▼

Answer: You can reach out to our customer support team through our Contact Us page or by emailing our support team. We will try to respond within 24 hours.

Do you add more activities as new ones come out? ▼

Answer: Yes! We continuously try to stay up to date and add the most interesting activities there is to in Kyoto as our top priority while also adding new lesser known activities that may interest different people as well!

Can I create multiple itineraries and be able to modify them? ▼

Answer: Of course! You can create multiple itinerary collections that you can organize and edit as you see fit for your trip! You are also able to delete any using our "Delete Itinerary" option.

What is the default itinerary? ▼

Answer: The default itinerary is your main itinerary where for any activity you click the "Add to Itinerary" button, it gets automatically added to your default itinerary where you can easily view and edit at any time. You can always change the dates and times after an activity gets added to your default itinerary. Make sure to have a default itinerary selected and have the right collection for it so you can add the activities you want to your desired itinerary collection!
