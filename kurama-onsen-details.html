Kurama Onsen

4.8 (200)

Kurama Onsen is a tranquil hot spring located in the mountains north of Kyoto, Japan, renowned for its stunning natural scenery and therapeutic waters. Nestled in the picturesque Kurama village, the onsen features both indoor and outdoor baths, allowing guests to soak while surrounded by lush forests and mountain views. The hot spring water is said to offer numerous health benefits, including relief from fatigue and muscle pain. Kurama Onsen is also notable for its traditional ryokan accommodations, where guests can enjoy authentic kaiseki cuisine made with local ingredients. ​The combination of serene natural beauty, soothing waters, and rich cultural experiences makes Kurama Onsen a perfect retreat for relaxation and rejuvenation​.

Details

Interest/Topic: Bathhouses/Onsens

Popularity: High

Reservation: Recommended for ryokan stays

Estimated Price Range: 1,000 - 3,500 yen ($9 - $32) for day-use; 10,000 - 25,000 yen ($90 - $225) for overnight stays

Tips

  1. Highlights: Explore Surrounding Areas: After your bath, consider wandering the nearby streets to experience local eateries or shops for a more immersive Kyoto experience.
  2. Duration: Year-round, particularly popular during colder months and in the warm hues of autumn.
  • Natural Hot Springs: Enjoy rejuvenating baths in beautiful mountain scenery.
  • Ryokan Experience: Stay in a ryokan with tatami rooms and kaiseki meals.
  • Scenic Location: Just 30 minutes from Kyoto, with walks to Kurama-dera Temple.
  • ​- Wellness Benefits: Hot springs relieve stress and muscle pain.​
  • Seasonal Events: Participate in cultural activities like night bathing.
  • Transportation: Take the Eizan Railway; 30 minutes from downtown Kyoto.
  • Hours and Fees: Check seasonal hours and entrance fees.
  • Bring Towels: Consider bringing your own for convenience.
  • Bathing Etiquette: Shower before entering; no towels in the baths.
  • ​Stay Overnight: Enjoy tranquility and traditional meals at a ryokan.
