Itinerary Name: Kyoto First Days

Destination(s): Kyoto City, Arashiyama, KameokaBudget: $378

Start Date: 1/1/2025End Date: 1/3/2025

Change Cover

Day 1

Gion District Starbucks

Kyoto City Food & Drink

Day 1 6:00AM-8:00AM

Notes: Get some Starbucks drinks in the morning then walk around the area and look around different shops and restaurants.

Day 2

No activities scheduled for this day

Day 3

Kinkakuji Temple

Kyoto City Landmarks

Day 3 12:00PM

Notes: Visit Kinkaku-ji, the Golden Pavilion, set amidst beautiful gardens and a reflective pond. Spend about 30 minutes to an hour exploring the temple and its serene surroundings. Best to visit early to avoid crowds.

Day 4

Arashiyama Bamboo Forest

Kyoto City Nature & Outdoors

Day 4 11:00AM-2:00PM

Notes: Explore the Arashiyama Bamboo Forest, a stunning path surrounded by towering bamboo. A 20-30 minute walk offers peaceful views. Visit early to avoid crowds and enjoy the serene atmosphere.

Unscheduled Activities

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Kyoto City Landmarks

Unscheduled Time

Notes: Walk through the iconic red Torii gates, visit the main shrine, and explore small shrines along the trail. The hike to the top takes 1.5 to 3 hours, offering beautiful views and fox statues. Wear comfortable shoes, bring water, and aim to visit early or late to avoid crowds.
